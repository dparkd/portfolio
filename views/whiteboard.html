Whiteboard
A critique web app

BACKGROUND


I created Whiteboard so that students who are in the creative field can share their projects and designs just like in the classroom. I find that the whiteboard critique is the most effective way to get constructive feedback.
This is a work in progress - I am currently in the development stage.
